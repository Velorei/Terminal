Summary	- "Никоим образом" item is incorrectly translated on the user registration page after changing the language to Russian				

Description	- "Никоим образом" item is incorrectly translated on the user registration page after changing the language to Russian				

Preconditions:	
  1. The site https://capital.com  is opened;
  2. The language is changed to Russian.			

Steps to reproduce:
  1. Click the "Создать аккаунт" button;
  2. Enter valid data in the "Адресс электронной почты" and "Пароль" fields;
  3. Click the "Продолжить" button;
  4. Click "Перейти на платформу" button;
  5. Mark the "Я не являюсь yалоговым резидентом США" checkbox;
  6. Click the "Пролоджить" button;
  7. Click any item in the "Причина открытия аккаунта" page;
  8. Pay attention to the "Никоим образом" item in the "У вас есть опыт торговли?" page.				

Actual result	- "Никоим образом" item is incorrectly translated on the user registration page after changing the language to Russian				

Expected result -	"Вовсе нет" item is translated on the user registration page after changing the language to Russian				

Additional Information - Reproduced also when Ukrainian language is selected
